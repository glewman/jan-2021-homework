Great Flowers for Beginners

There are so many choices for the beginner to choose from. When trying to decide what flowers are best for you, there are two main factors you need to keep in mind. First, what region of the country you live in. Here is a link that will let you know what zone you are in. Second is where in your yard you want to plant your flowers and how much sunlight those areas receive.

Here is a list of perennial flowers:

  • Lilies

  • Tulips

  • Irises

Here is a list of annual flowers:

  1. Vincas

  2. Calibrachoa

  3. Petunias

Finally, there is so much information about the different conditions and levels of care needed for both perennials and annuals. Here is a good link.
